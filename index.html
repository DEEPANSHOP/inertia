inertia is a multi-genre imprint dedicated to the rawest sounds of the underground. Specializing heavily in Brazilian Funk and Phonk, we exist to amplify artists who break the mold and refuse to conform to algorithmic standards.

Founded by producers, for producers. We understand the grind of the independent scene because we live it. We know what it takes to cut through the noise, and our mission is to provide a platform that elevates authentic, boundary-pushing electronic music.
